What the Club actually means.

One injector, always.

Hannah Morse, RN treats you at every visit. No rotating cast, no handoffs — your face has one artist of record.

Appointments, not lines.

By appointment only means your time is protected and your visit is never rushed. The studio is yours while you're in it.

Plans, not pushes.

Results designed to last, mapped and priced before anything happens. If a treatment won't serve you, it stays on the menu.

Your injector

Meet Hannah Morse, RN.

Hannah built The Aesthetics Club around a simple frustration: aesthetics had become an assembly line. Her answer is a studio where the person who consults you is the person who treats you — a registered nurse whose name is quite literally on the practice (injectionsbyhannah).

Every appointment starts with a conversation and ends with a plan designed to last — not a punch card of upsells.
