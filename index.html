Straight answers.

Do ads change what ChatGPT says?

No. Ads don't alter the assistant's organic answers — they appear clearly labeled as sponsored, below the response. That's also why we pair paid placement with organic AI visibility work: the answer itself still matters.

Which users actually see these ads?

Ads currently show to Free and Go tier users in the US, Canada, Australia and New Zealand, with OpenAI expanding to the UK, Mexico, Brazil, Japan and South Korea. Paid-tier subscribers don't see ads.

What budget do I need?

The pilot fee is $1,800 flat. For ad spend, we recommend a $1,500–3,000 test budget over the 30 days, paid directly on your own card. There's no platform minimum — CPCs are running around $3–5.

What's the difference between GEO and paid ads?

Paid ads buy labeled placement in relevant conversations — fast, measurable, off the moment you stop. GEO (generative engine optimization) earns organic citations in the AI's actual answers — slower, compounding. We lead with paid because it's measurable in 30 days; GEO reviews are included in ongoing management.

Do you lock me into a contract?

No. The audit is free, the pilot is a one-time flat fee, and management is month-to-month — cancel anytime. We also never hold your ad budget; spend goes on your card, in your own ad account, which you keep.

How do you measure results?

Through OpenAI's Conversions API wired to your Shopify revenue — not clicks or impressions. Every weekly email shows spend, revenue attributed, and what we're changing next. If the numbers don't justify scaling after 30 days, we'll say so.
